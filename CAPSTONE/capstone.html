What is an Attention Seeker?

Attention Seeker is a smart app that will sound the alarm whenever a User falls asleep while reading. Most of the time, on exam days students study for a long time. Some time while studying our eyes close unintentionally. When they awake, it was too late to complete the whole syllabus. And he doesn’t know how to complete whole the syllabus and therefore he takes the stress and due to this, it became a burden on his mind and he doesn’t know what he should be done. due to mental stress, he feels laziness and uncomfortable and when he appears in the exam paper due to stress he can’t attempt the paper truly and due to this he finally fails. So to avoid this, we are creating an app that will sound the alarm whenever you fall asleep while reading. So that we should wake up because of this alarm and complete our course by focusing on study so that we can avoid mental stress .

System Requirements

Bluetooth Speackers (Optional).

Since the mobile application hasn't any designated hardware except the android device itself, it does not have any direct hardware interfaces. However, in case of alarming the user via Bluetooth, Bluetooth speakers would be required to connect with and alarm the user.

Above API 26.

This application would better work upon those devices that are having a fine camera to detect at least the eye curves. Nowadays, every mobile phone contains a higher quality camera that can easily detect the curves alone and process those inputs efficiently as they are intelligent too. So, we are assuming that it would work efficiently above API 26 i.e., Android Oreo, while getting maximal output and accuracy for the proper functioning of our application.

Proper Light Mechanism

This application needs enough light mechanism to detect the eye patterns of the user. So proper light mechanisms should be available for those users who are intended to use the Attention Seeker Application while particularly using it at night. In daylight, there is no need for any lights because even indirect sunlight is sufficient for detecting eye patterns by the Camera sensor, so precisely, the limit is biased towards those users who are going to use this at night.

Fine camera

Camera should be of high mega-pixels for easily capturing the eye pattern.

STEP 1

When the user prompts for a "Start" button, it will trigger our primary detection function.

STEP 2

the Camera will be launched to read the user's face. The Camera will start reading eye patterns.

STEP 3

. If the received patterns are similar to the open eye's curve, it is assumed that the student is awaking. Otherwise, if the patterns are not enough to look like the curve of the eye or those patterns are curved close enough to satisfy the state of sleepiness, i.e., the curve is tangent close to the straight line; then the student would be assumed in an unsafe condition.

STEP 4

After that, a popup message containing the alarm message will be appeared for as much time as the user intentionally cancel it to stop the alarm.

FINISH

After pressing the Stop button, the Camera will again start monitoring the reader eye's based on previous configurations. Moreover, if the reader wishes for new configurations or customization, he will prompt back to the options to set them respectively
